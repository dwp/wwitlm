1. How it works

You can apply for a Budgeting Loan to help pay for:

  • furniture or household items like white goods
  • clothes or footwear
  • rent in advance
  • costs linked to moving house
  • maintenance, improvements or security for your home
  • travelling costs within the UK
  • costs linked to getting a new job
  • maternity costs
  • funeral costs
  • repaying hire purchase loans
  • repaying loans taken for the above items

If you apply online, you’ll be told whether you’ve been offered a loan within 12 days. If you choose to accept a loan offer, you’ll receive the money within 5 days. The loan is paid into your bank, building society or credit union account.

If you get Universal Credit, apply for a budgeting advance from your local Jobcentre Plus instead.
