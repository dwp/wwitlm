4. Check your eligibility and apply

To get a Budgeting Loan, you must have been getting one of these benefits for the past 6 months:

  • Income Support
  • income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • income-related Employment and Support Allowance
  • Pension Credit

You may also be eligible if you’ve had a combination of these benefits or have had a short breaks in your benefit of up to 28 days.

You can’t get a Budgeting Loan if:

  • you’re involved in industrial action (for example, a strike, walkout or lockout)
  • you owe more than £1,500 for Crisis Loans and Budgeting Loans

You will need

Before you apply make sure you have:

  • your National Insurance number
  • details about your regular debt repayments (such as hire purchases and loans, but not normal living expenses like gas and electric charges or food bills)

Other ways to apply

You can get an application form at your local Jobcentre Plus. If you’d like one posted to you call 0345 603 6967 between 8am and 6pm, Monday to Friday (charges apply).
