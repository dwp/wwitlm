What happens after applying

If you’re offered a loan you’ll be told:

  • how much you can borrow
  • how much your weekly repayments will be
  • how long it will take to repay

You can then decide whether to accept the loan.

If you’re not offered a loan, then you’ll be told the reason why. You have the right to ask for a review if you’re not happy with the decision.

Declaration

By sending this application you agree:

  • the information in this application is complete and correct as far as you know
  • to report any changes to your circumstances promptly
  • that you'll use the loan for one of the qualifying reasons
See the qualifying reasons for a loan

You can use a Budgeting Loan to pay for:

  • furniture or household items such as white goods
  • clothing or footwear
  • rent in advance
  • costs linked to moving house
  • maintenance, security or improvements to your home
  • travelling costs within the UK
  • costs linked to getting a new job
  • maternity costs
  • funeral costs
  • repaying hire purchase loans
  • repaying loans taken for the above items

You may be taken to court if you deliberately give wrong information or if you don’t report changes to your circumstances promptly.
