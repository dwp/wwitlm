About us

We’re a small team of analytical and policy staff in The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) working with internal and external experts to understand labour market evidence.

DWP wants to make it easier for policy and operational decision makers to use evidence in designing and delivering services.

To make it easier for decision makers to use evidence, on this site we are:

  • summarising evidence on active labour market policies
  • sharing this in user-friendly formats and maintaining and updating this on an on-going basis
  • maintaining a research library of the most relevant reports
  • mapping live external research/trials to make these more visible to all
  • holding case studies showing examples of effective local practice
