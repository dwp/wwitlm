Basic skills

Last updated 10 October 2016

What is it?

Wage subsidies are payments made to employers or employees and incentivise hiring.

Why should this work?

Subsidising wages increases employer demand for the subsidised worker, i.e. the unemployed. In addition the unemployed (especially young & LTU) may be perceived as a risky choice for an employer; the wage subsidy compensates the employer for this risk.
